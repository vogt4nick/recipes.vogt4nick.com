---
title: Kewpie-style vinaigrette
---

=== "Ingredients"

    - 30 g Neutral Oil
    - 1/4 t MSG or Medium-Grain Salt
    - 1 t Dijon Mustard
    - 1 t Hot Sauce
    - 1 t Lemon Juice or Rice Vinegar
    - 2 t Rice Vinegar

    !!! info "Yields about 50 g 2:1 vinaigrette."

=== "Directions"

    Combine ingredients and mix well. Refrigerate to store.
