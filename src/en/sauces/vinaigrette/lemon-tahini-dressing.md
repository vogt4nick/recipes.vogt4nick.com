=== "Ingredients"

    - 60 g [Tahini Paste](../tahini-paste.md)
    - 45 g Lemon Juice, fresh
    - 2 Garlic Cloves, finely grated
    - 45 g Cold Water
    - 1/2 t Medium-Grain Salt
    - 1/4 t Cumin Seed, ground

=== "Directions"

    1. **Combine base.** Whisk together tahini, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and cumin
        in a bowl or jar. The mixture will seize and thicken -- that is normal.
    2. **Thin to consistency.** Add cold water one tablespoon at a time, whisking
        after each addition, until the dressing is smooth and pourable.
    3. **Taste and adjust** salt and lemon to preference.
    4. **Store** in a sealed jar in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. Stir in a
        splash of cold water to loosen before serving.

    !!! info "Tahini seizes before it smooths.[^kassem]"

        Tahini is a fat-continuous emulsion. Adding a water-based liquid such as lemon
        juice initiates a transition toward a water-continuous emulsion; the seizing and
        stiffening occurs in the transitional state between the two. Continued addition
        of water completes the inversion, yielding a smooth sauce.

!!! tip "Use on grain salads, roasted vegetables, and grilled meats."
