=== "Ingredients"

    - 80 g Olive Oil
    - 40 g Apple Cider Vinegar
    - 8 g Ground Cumin
    - 4 g Garlic Powder
    - Salt
    - Black Peppercorn, ground

    !!! info "Yields approx 120 g."

=== "Directions"

    Combine dressing ingredients in a sealable jar. Shake vigorously to emulsify.

!!! tip "Ideal for grain salads and meat-heavy salads."
