=== "Ingredients"

    - 60 g [Tahini Paste](tahini-paste.md)
    - 45 g Lemon Juice
    - 2 Garlic Cloves, grated
    - 1/2 t Medium-Grain Salt
    - 1/4 t Cumin Seed, ground
    - 3 T Cold Water

=== "Directions"

    1. **Combine base.** Whisk tahini, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and cumin. The
        mixture will seize and thicken.
    2. **Thin.** Add cold water one tablespoon at a time, whisking after each
        addition, until the dressing is smooth and pourable.
    3. **Season to taste** with additional salt and lemon.

    ??? info "Tahini seizes before it smooths.[^kassem]"

        Tahini is a fat-continuous emulsion. Adding a water-based liquid such as lemon
        juice initiates a transition toward a water-continuous emulsion; the seizing and
        stiffening occurs in the transitional state between the two. Continued addition
        of water completes the inversion, yielding a smooth sauce.

    !!! info "Store chilled in a sealed jar up to 5 days."

        Stir in a splash of cold water to loosen before serving.

!!! tip "Use on grain salads, roasted vegetables, and grilled meats."

[^kassem]: Kassem, Jessica M.
