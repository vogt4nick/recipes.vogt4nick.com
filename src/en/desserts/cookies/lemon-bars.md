=== "Ingredients"

    - 240 g White Wheat Flour
    - 240 g Unsalted Butter
    - 100 g Powdered Sugar
    - 4 Eggs, beaten
    - 100 g Lemon Juice
    - 400 g White Sugar
    - 30 g White Wheat Flour
    - 1/2 t Medium-Grain Salt
    - To Garnish
        - Powdered Sugar

=== "Directions"

    1. **Preheat oven** to 175ºC (350ºF).
    1. **Mix crust.** Beat flour, butter, and powdered sugar into a creamy dough.
    1. **Bake crust.** Press crust into an ungreased baking pan, and bake 20 minutes
        at 175ºC (350ºF).
    1. **Mix filling.** Combine beaten eggs, lemon juice, sugar, flour, and salt.
    1. **Bake bars.** Pour filling onto baked crust, and bake 25 minutes at 175ºC
        (350ºF).
    1. **Garnish** with powdered sugar and serve.
