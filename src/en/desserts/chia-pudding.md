=== "Ingredients"

    - 10 g Chia Seeds
    - 50 g Water
    - 100 g Frozen Blueberries
    - 200 g Skyr

    !!! info "Yields 1 serving."

=== "Directions"

    1. **Combine** chia, water, and frozen blueberries.
    2. **Thaw and soak** 2-12 hours.
    3. **Mix** in skyr and serve.
