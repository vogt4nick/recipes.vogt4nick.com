=== "Ingredients"

    - 200 g Dry Whole Spelt Berries
    - 600 g Water or [Stock](../../soups/stocks/vegetable-stock.md)

    !!! info "Yields approx 600 g cooked."

=== "Directions"

    1. **Rinse** spelt berries.
    2. **Soak (optional)** 8 hours.
    3. **Cook.** Bring to boil, reduce heat, and cover. Simmer 30-50 minutes (45-90
        minutes if not soaked) until tender and liquid is mostly absorbed.
    4. **Drain** any remaining liquid.
    5. **Fluff** gently with a fork.
