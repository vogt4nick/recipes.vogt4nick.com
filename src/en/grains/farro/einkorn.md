=== "Ingredients"

    - 200 g Dry Whole Einkorn Berries
    - 600 g Water or [Stock](../../soups/stocks/vegetable-stock.md)

    !!! info "Yields approx 600 g cooked."

=== "Directions"

    1. **Rinse** farro berries.
    2. **Cook.** Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer 25 minutes until
        tender and liquid is mostly absorbed.
    3. **Drain** any remaining liquid.
    4. **Fluff** gently with a fork.
