# Millet rice

=== "Ingredients"

    - 150 g Dry Basmati Rice
    - 50 g Dry Millet
    - Cool water, for rinsing and soaking
    - 425 g Water
    - 1/2 t Medium-Grain Salt

=== "Directions"

    1. **Rinse** rice several times until water runs mostly clear.
    1. **Soak** rice 20 minutes.
    1. **Toast millet** (optional) in a saucepan over medium-low heat 4-6 minutes
        until golden and aromatic.
    1. **Cook.** Add rice, salt, and water to a saucepan. Bring to boil, cover,
        reduce, and simmer 20 minutes. Finally, while covered, remove pan from heat
        and let stand for 10 minutes.
    1. **Fluff** rice with a fork.
    1. **Cool and serve.** Allow rice to cool, uncovered, for a few minutes before
        serving.

    !!! caution "**Do not** uncover the rice while cooking."
