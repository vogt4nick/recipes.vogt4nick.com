=== "Ingredients"

    - 25 g Fermented Rye Malt (Roggenbackmalz)
    - 1000 g Water, hot (~85ºC)
    - 1 T [Sourdough Starter](../breads/leaven-breads/starter.md) or 1 t Dry Active
        Yeast
    - 60 g Water, lukewarm (~40ºC)
    - 60 g White Sugar, granulated

=== "Directions"

    1. **Make wort.** Whisk rye malt into hot water until fully dissolved. Cool to
        below 40ºC (104ºF).
    2. **Activate yeast.** Combine yeast and lukewarm water. Stir and rest 10
        minutes until foamy.
    3. **Combine.** Stir sugar into cooled wort until dissolved. Add yeast starter
        and stir to combine. Pour into sterilized fermentation jar.
    4. **Apply airlock.**
    5. **Ferment** at 20-26ºC (68-79ºF) in a dark place 2-4 days. Taste daily from
        day 2 until tangy and lightly sweet.
    6. **Cold crash.** Move jar to refrigerator without disturbing it. Rest 2 days
        until sediment settles.
    7. **Rack.** Carefully pour clear kvass off sediment into a clean bottle or jug,
        leaving the cloudy layer behind.
    8. **Serve cold.**

    !!! tip "A balloon is a cheap airlock."

        It allows off-gassing carbon dioxide without allowing new oxygen in.

!!! info "Store up to 1 week in refrigerator."
