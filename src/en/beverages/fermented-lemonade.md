=== "Ingredients"

    - 110 g White Granulated Sugar
    - 890 g Water
    - 1/4 t Dry Active Yeast
    - 15 g Lemon Juice

    !!! info "Yields approx 1 liter ~5.5% ABV lemonade."

=== "Directions"

    1. **Sterilize** a sealable container.
    2. **Dissolve** sugar in warm water.
    3. **Flavor** with lemon juice.
    4. **Ferment.** Stir in dry active yeast.
    5. **Apply airlock.**
    6. **Ferment** 7-10 days at room temperature.
    7. **Chill** 2 days until clear.
    8. **Serve.**

    !!! tip "A balloon is a cheap airlock."

        It allows off-gassing carbon dioxide without allowing new oxygen in.
