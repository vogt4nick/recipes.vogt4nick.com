---
title: Crème fraîche
---
=== "Ingredients"
    * 500 g Heavy Cream
    * 50 g Cultured Buttermilk

=== "Directions"
    1. **Mix** ingredients together.
    2. **Rest** 24 hours, covered, in a warm spot until thick (75ºF or 22ºC).
    3. **Chill** 24 hours, covered, before using.

!!! info "Store up to two weeks chilled."
