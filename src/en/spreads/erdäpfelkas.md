# Erdäpfelkas (potato cheese)

=== "Ingredients"

    - 700 g Potatoes, peeled
    - 150 g Quark (Topfen), drained
    - 60 g Sour Cream
    - 1 T Unsalted Butter, room temperature
    - 1 Yellow Onion, minced
    - 2 Garlic Clove, minced
    - 1 t Caraway Seed, ground
    - 1 t Medium-Grain Salt
    - 1 t Black Peppercorn, ground
    - For Garnish
        - Oil
        - Paprika
        - Fresh Chives, minced
    - For Serving
        - Rye Bread
        - Radishes
        - Cucumber
        - Carrot sticks

    !!! tip "Drain quark overnight if wet. Wet quark makes a loose spread.[^wieser]"

=== "Directions"

    1. **Steam potatoes** 25 minutes until completely tender when pierced.
    2. **Puree potatoes.** While hot, rice or mash potatoes until smooth. Let cool
        to warm.
    3. **Mix dairy.** Separately, stir together quark, sour cream, butter, onion,
        garlic, and caraway until smooth.
    4. **Fold** dairy mixture into warm potatoes until spreadable. Season to taste.
    5. **Garnish and serve.**

    !!! tip "To avoid gluey texture, do not use an electric mixer or food processor."

!!! info "Store chilled; bring to room temperature 20 minutes before serving."
