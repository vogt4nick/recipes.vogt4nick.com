=== "Ingredients"

    - 200 g Quark, Topfen, or Low-Fat Cottage Cheese
    - 40 g Hard Cheese, thickly grated
    - 80 g Butter, room temperature
    - 1 T Sweet Paprika
    - 1/2 T Dijon Mustard
    - 1 t Caraway Seeds, ground
    - 1 Onion, finely minced
    - 3 small Gherkins, minced
    - 1 T Capers, minced
    - Salt
    - Black Peppercorn, ground
    - Garnish
        - Chives, thinly sliced

=== "Directions"

    1. **Combine** all ingredients.
    2. **Chill**, covered, at least 1 hour.
    3. **Garnish and serve.**

!!! info "Store chilled; bring to room temperature 20 minutes before serving."
