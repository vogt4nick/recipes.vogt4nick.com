=== "Ingredients"

    - 2 T Olive Oil
    - 1 Onion, minced
    - Garlic, minced
    - 1 t Paprika
    - 1/2 t Thyme
    - 1/2 t Rosemary
    - 250 g Vegetable Stock
    - 125 g Milk
    - 500 g Cooked White Beans
    - 200 g Frozen Spinach
    - 1 Lemon Zest
    - Nutritional Yeast
    - For Serving
        - Black Peppercorn, ground
        - Salt
        - Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated

    !!! info "Yields 4 servings."

=== "Directions"

    1. **Sweat base.** Cook onion and garlic 5 minutes over medium heat until soft.
        Add spices and cook 30 seconds until fragrant. Add vegetable stock and
        milk. Stir to distribute spices.
    2. **Simmer.** Add beans and spinach. Cook until heated evenly.
    3. **Season** with lemon zest and nutritional yeast.

!!! tip "Add 250 g starchy pasta water and serve with pasta."
