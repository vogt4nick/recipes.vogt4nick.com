=== "Ingredients"

    - 2 Eggs
    - 120 g Milk
    - Medium-Grain Salt
    - 1 t Vanilla Extract
    - 1/2 t Cinnamon, ground
    - 1/4 t Allspice Corns, ground
    - Stale Bread, thickly sliced
    - 2 T Unsalted Butter, for frying
    - Maple Syrup or Jam, for serving

=== "Directions"

    1. **Preheat oven** to 200ºC (400ºF).
    1. **Mix batter.** Thoroughly whisk together eggs, milk, and seasonings.
    1. **Soak bread slices** in the batter.
    1. **Fry bread slices.** Heat butter in a frying pan over medium heat. Add
        saturated bread slices, and cook about 6 minutes, flipping halfwar, until
        golden brown.
    1. **Bake.** Transfer slices to a baking sheet, and bake 12-15 minutes at 200ºC
        (400ºF).
    1. **Serve** with maple syrup or jam.

    !!! tip "Do your best to fully saturate the bread such that a tiny bit of batter is left over."
