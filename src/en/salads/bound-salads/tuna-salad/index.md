# Tuna salad

=== "Ingredients"

    - 100 g [Mayonnaise](../../../sauces/hollandaise/mayonnaise/index.md)
    - 1/4 t Medium-Grain Salt
    - 1 t Dijon Mustard
    - 1 t Hot Sauce
    - 2 t Lemon Juice
    - 1 t White Wine Vinegar
    - 2 t Dried Dill Leaves
    - 1/4 t Dried Marjoram Leaves
    - 1/4 t Dried Oregano Leaves
    - 1/4 t Dried Thyme Leaves
    - 1/4 t Garlic Powder
    - 200 g Canned Tuna

=== "Directions"

    Combine all ingredients except tuna, and whisk until smooth. Mix in tuna.
