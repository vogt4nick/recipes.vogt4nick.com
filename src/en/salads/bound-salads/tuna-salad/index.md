# Tuna salad

=== "Ingredients"

    - 150 g Canned Tuna, drained
    - Dressing
        - 30 g [Mayonnaise](../../../sauces/hollandaise/mayonnaise/index.md) or Oil
        - 1 T Lemon Juice
        - 1 t Dijon Mustard
        - 1/2 t Kosher Salt
        - 1/4 t Black Peppercorn, ground
        - 2 t Dried Dill Leaves or Dried Chives
    - 1 Celery Stalk, sliced
    - 1 Red Bell Pepper, minced
    - 1 Carrot, shredded
    - 50 g [Dry Barley, cooked](../../../grains/barley/index.md) (optional)

=== "Directions"

    1. **Mix dressing.**
    2. **Mix in tuna.** Let soak a minute or two.
    3. **Mix in vegetables.**
    4. **Mix in barley.**
