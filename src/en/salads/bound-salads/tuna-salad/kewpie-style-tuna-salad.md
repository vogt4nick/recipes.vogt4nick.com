# Kewpie-style tuna salad

=== "Ingredients"

    - 50 g
        [Kewpie-Style Vinaigrette](../../../sauces/vinaigrette/mustard-based/kewpie-style-vinaigrette.md)
    - 2 t Dried Dill Leaves
    - 1/4 t Dried Marjoram Leaves
    - 1/4 t Dried Oregano Leaves
    - 1/4 t Dried Thyme Leaves
    - 1/4 t Garlic Powder
    - 150 g Canned Tuna
    - 130 g Canned Edamame
    - 50 g [Dry Barley, cooked](../../../grains/barley/index.md) (optional)

    !!! info "Yields 1 serving."

=== "Directions"

    1. **Mix vinaigrette.** Combine vinaigrette and herbs.
    2. **Soak tuna.** Mix to combine tuna and edamame.
    3. **Rest** a minute to allow garlic powder to hydrate and tuna to absorb
        flavors.
    4. **Add barley.**
