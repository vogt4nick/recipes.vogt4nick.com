=== "Ingredients"

    - 500 g Dry Pasta, cooked
    - 300 g Cooked Sausage, roughly chopped
    - 200 g Pickles, roughly chopped
    - 1 Red Bell Pepper, roughly chopped
    - 150 g Frozen Peas, thawed
    - 140 g Sweetcorn, drained
    - 3 [Hardboiled Eggs](../../../eggs/hardboiled-eggs.md), cut into wedges
    - Dressing
        - 250 g [Mayonnaise](../../../sauces/hollandaise/mayonnaise/index.md)
        - 100 g Sour Cream or Plain Yogurt
        - 2 t Mustard
        - 75 g Pickle Brine
        - 1 t Sugar
        - Black Peppercorn, ground
        - Fresh Flat-Leaf Parsley Leaves, chopped
        - Fresh Chives, chopped

    !!! info "Yields 6-8 side servings"

=== "Directions"

    1. **Make dressing.** Whisk dressing ingredients.
    2. **Season to taste.** Dressing may be quite salty already from pickle brine.
        Salt only as necessary.
    3. **Combine.** Add cooled pasta to dressing and stir to coat. Mix to combine
        remaining ingredients.
    4. **Chill** at least 8 hours.
