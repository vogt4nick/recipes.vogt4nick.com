# Bean salad

=== "Ingredients"

    - Vinaigrette
        - 80 g Olive Oil
        - 30 g Red Wine Vinegar
        - 10 g Lemon Juice
        - 1/2 t Medium-Grain Salt
        - 1/2 t Black Peppercorn, ground
    - 40 g Shallots or Red Onion
    - 400 g [Dry Legumes, slightly undercooked](../../legumes/beans/index.md), warm
    - Garnish
        - Fresh Parsley Leaves, chopped

    !!! info "This recipes works for all legumes."

    ??? tip "7 Simple Last-Minute Additions to Bean Salads[^bittman]"

        1. Chopped fresh, dried, or oven-roasted tomatoes.
        1. Raw or
           [roasted](../../vegetables/oven-roasted-vegetables/oven-roasted-bell-peppers.md)
           chopped peppers or chilis.
        1. Chopped salad greens like romaine, arugula, spinach, etc.
        1. Cooked greens like kale, chard, spinach, broccoli, bok choy, green beans,
           etc.
        1. Chopped cooked mushrooms.
        1. Grated, shaved, or cubed cheese, like Parmesan, blue, feta, queso fresco,
           Jack, or cheddar.
        1. Crumbled or chopped crisp bacon, sausage, or chunk tuna.

=== "Directions"

    1. **Combine.** Add onion, red wine vinegar, olive oil, salt, pepper to large
        bowl. Stir to combine. Add legumes. Toss gently.
    1. **Cool** to room temperature, gently stirring every 20 minutes.
    1. **Garnish & serve** with parsley.
