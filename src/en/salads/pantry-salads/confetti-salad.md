=== "Ingredients"

    - 160 g Quinoa, dry, rinsed, soaked 2 hours, drained
    - 80 g Millet, dry, soaked 2 hours, drained
    - 80 g Bulgur, dry
    - Vegetable Stock
    - 120 g [Cumin Vinaigrette](../../sauces/vinaigrette/cumin-vinaigrette.md)
    - 480 g Black Beans, canned, drained (2 cans)
    - 285 g Corn Kernels, canned, drained (1 can)
    - 160 g Green Peas, frozen
    - 160 g Carrots, raw, grated
    - 80 g Scallions, sliced, blanched

=== "Directions"

    1. **Cook bulgur** in vegetable stock
        [per bulgur directions](../../grains/bulgur.md).
    2. **Cook quinoa and millet** in vegetable stock
        [per quinoa directions](../../grains/quinoa/index.md).
    3. **Mix grains.** Combine bulgur, quinoa, millet, and vinaigrette.
    4. **Add remaining solids.** Add beans, corn, peas, carrots, and scallions. Mix
        to combine.
