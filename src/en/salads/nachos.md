=== "Ingredients"

    - Tortilla Chips
    - Cheddar Cheese, divided into 2 equal portions
    - Ground Beef, cooked, fat drained, seasoned
    - [Refried Beans](../legumes/beans/refried-beans.md), thinned with splash of
        water until spreadable
    - For Serving
        - Sour Cream
        - Guacamole or Avocado, sliced
        - Pico de Gallo or Fresh Tomato
        - Jalapeño
        - Fresh Cilantro Leaves

    ??? tip "Beef should be dry and crumbly."

        Any excess fat or cooking liquid will steam chips from above.

    ??? tip "Don't skip thinning the beans."

        Thick refried beans applied directly to chips are the primary cause of soggy
        nachos. A loose, spreadable consistency distributes more evenly and reduces
        pooling moisture.

=== "Directions"

    1. **Preheat oven** to 190ºC (375ºF). Foil a sheet pan.
    2. **Build cheese barrier.** Spread chips in a single, even layer on the sheet
        pan. Sprinkle first portion of cheese over every chip. Bake 4 minutes until
        cheese just melts. Remove from oven.
    3. **Dress nachos.** Dollop and spread thinned refried beans across the chip
        layer. Distribute ground beef evenly on top. Sprinkle second portion of
        cheese over everything.
    4. **Bake** 7 minutes until top cheese is fully melted and edges of chips are
        just golden. Watch closely! They turn fast.
    5. **Top & serve immediately.** Add cold toppings straight from the oven and
        serve at once.
