=== "Ingredients"

    - 400 g Strawberries, halved
    - 1 T Balsamic Vinegar
    - Black Peppercorn, ground
    - 120 g Goat Cheese, crumbled (optional)
    - 80 g Arugula Leaves
    - Salt
    - 1 T Olive Oil

=== "Directions"

    1. **Season strawberries.** Toss strawberries with balsamic and black pepper,
        and rest 10 minutes.
    1. **Combine.** Add arugula and salt and toss. Add goat cheese and toss. Drizzle
        with olive oil and toss again.
    1. **Season to taste** and serve.
