=== "Ingredients"

    - 600 g 80/20 Ground Beef
    - 2 Bell Peppers, minced
    - Kohlrabi, peeled and sliced into thin half-moons
    - Seasonings
        - 1 t Black Peppercorn
        - 1/2 t Smoked Paprika
        - 1/2 t Sweet Paprika
        - 1/2 t Hot Paprika
        - 1/2 t Garlic Powder
        - 1/2 t Onion Powder
        - 1/4 t Medium-Grain Salt
        - 1/4 t MSG
    - Sauce
        - 1 T Worcestershire Sauce
        - 1 T Hot Sauce
        - 1 T Shaoxing Wine or Apple Cider Vinegar
    - For Serving
        - [Japanese Rice](../../grains/rice/japanese-rice/index.md)

    !!! info "Yields 3-4 servings"

=== "Japanese Ingredients"

    - 600 g 80/20 Ground Beef
    - Seasonings
        - 1 t Black Peppercorn, ground
        - 1/2 t Garlic Powder
        - 1/2 t Ginger Powder
        - 1/4 t Medium-Grain Salt
        - 1/4 t MSG
    - Sauce
        - 2 T Soy Sauce
        - 1 T Mirin
        - 1 T Shaoxing Wine

    !!! info "Yields 3-4 servings"

=== "Directions"

    1. **Brown beef.** Preheat skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef and
        seasonings, stir to combine, and cook 20 minutes until thoroughly brown.
    2. **Deglaze.** Pour sauce, scrape up the fond, and stir to combine.
    3. **Saute vegetables.** Arrange meat on the edges of the pan. Add vegetables,
        and saute 5 minutes until cooked. Mix with brown beef. Remove from heat.
    4. **Serve warm** with rice.
