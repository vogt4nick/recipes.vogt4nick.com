=== "Ingredients"

    - Raw Chicken Breast, boneless, skinless
    - [Chicken Stock](../../soups/stocks/meat-stock.md) or Water, cool or room
        temperature
    - Herbs (optional)

=== "Directions"

    1. **Bring up to heat.** Put ingredients in heavy-bottomed stockpot. Heat until
        water temperature reads 80ºC (175ºF). Reduce heat and partially cover to
        maintain temperature.
    1. **Poach chicken** 10 minutes. Remove stockpot from heat and let sit 15
        minutes until internal temperature of the breasts reads 68ºC (155ºF).
    1. **Remove chicken.**

!!! info "Store up to 3 days chilled in an airtight container."
