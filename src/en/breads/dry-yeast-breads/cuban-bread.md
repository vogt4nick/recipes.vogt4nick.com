=== "Ingredients"

    - Starter
        - 120 g Water, warm
        - 60 g White Wheat Flour
        - 1/4 t Active Dry Yeast
    - 7 g Active Dry Yeast
    - 2 t White Sugar
    - 180 g Water, warm
    - 40 g Lard
    - 2 t Medium-Grain Salt, fine
    - 360 g White Wheat Flour

=== "Baker's Percentage"

    | Ingredient | Starter | Dough | Total | Baker's Percentage |
    | :- | -: | -: | -: | -: |
    | White Wheat Flour | 60 g | 360 g | 420 g | 100 % |
    | Water | 120 g | 180 g | 300 g | 64 % |
    | Medium-Grain Salt |  | 10 g | 10 g | 2 % |
    | Active Dry Yeast | 0.25 g | 7 g | 7.25 g | 1.73 % |

=== "Directions"

    1. **Mix starter**. Mix to combine starter ingredients. Refrigerate overnight
        (or at least 6 hours).
    1. **Mix dough**. Mix to combine remaining water, sugar, yeast, starter, lard,
        and salt. Dividing as necessary, add flour and mix until a dough ball
        forms.
    1. **Knead dough**. Transfer dough to a floured surface. Knead 7-10 minutes,
        adding flour as necessary, until a soft, slightly tacky dough forms.
    1. **Proof dough**. Transfer kneaded dough to an oiled bowl. Cover. Rest 2 hours
        until dough has doubled in size.
    1. **Form loaves**. Transfer proofed dough to a floured surface. Dividing into
        two halves, flatten and shape proofed dough into a roughly 30 cm long
        rectangle and roll tightly into a loaf. Flatten slightly, if desired.
    1. **Proof loaves**. Transfer loaves to a sheet pan dressed with parchment paper
        and semolina. Cover and proof about 2 hours until doubled in size.
    1. **Preheat oven** to 400ºF (205ºC).
    1. **Dress loaves**. Uncover. Slice loaves lengthwise with a knife or
        razorblade. Spritz with water.
    1. **Bake loaves** 25 minutes at 400ºF. Remove from heat.
    1. **Rest loaves** 15 minutes.
