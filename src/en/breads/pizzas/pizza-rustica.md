=== "Ingredients"

    - Crusts
        - 300 g White Wheat Flour
        - 1 t Medium-Grain Salt
        - 120 g Butter, very cold, cut into pieces
        - 2 T Olive Oil
        - 1 Egg, beaten
        - 60 g Water, cold
    - 480 g Ricotta Cheese, drained
    - 240 g Low-Moisture Mozzarella Cheese, chopped
    - 60 g Pecorino Romano Cheese, grated
    - 1/2 t Medium-Grain Salt
    - 1 t Black Peppercorn, ground
    - 7 Eggs, beaten
    - 360 g Italian Sausage, cooked, crumbled
    - 120 g Smoked Ham, chopped
    - 120 g Salami, chopped
    - 120 g Pepperoni, cut into strips
    - For Egg Wash
        - 1 Egg
        - 1 t Water

    !!! info "Yields 8 servings."

=== "Directions"

    1. **Make crust dough.** Combine flour, salt, butter, and olive oil in a large
        bowl. Blend butter into flour with a pastry cutter or fingertips until it
        resembles coarse crumbs. Make a well in center; add egg and mix with a
        fork 1 minute until incorporated. Add cold water and mix briefly. Switch
        to hands and press into a ball. Sprinkle in more water 1 t at a time if
        dry until dough comes together.
    2. **Chill dough.** Press into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate at
        least 1 hour.
    3. **Preheat oven** to 220ºC (450ºF). Oil a 9-inch springform pan and dust with
        flour.
    4. **Make filling.** Thoroughly mix all filling ingredients in a large bowl.
    5. **Line pan with dough.** Cut off 1/3 of dough and set aside for top crust.
        Roll remaining dough into a 38 cm (15 in) circle, about 3 mm (1/8 in)
        thick. Transfer to pan and tuck into bottom and up sides without
        stretching. Patch any gaps with dampened excess dough.
    6. **Add filling.** Transfer filling to pan and press firmly into edges to
        eliminate air pockets. Smooth top.
    7. **Top with dough.** Roll reserved dough into a circle slightly wider than
        pan. Transfer to pan and press to seal with side edges.
    8. **Apply egg wash.** Whisk egg wash. Brush top crust. Trim excess dough if
        desired. Fold side dough onto top crust and crimp until sealed. Brush
        again with egg wash.
    9. **Bake.** Place in center of oven and immediately reduce heat to 175ºC
        (350ºF). Bake 1h 15m until top crust is golden brown.
    10. **Cool.** Rest 10 minutes. Remove springform ring and cool another 15
        minutes.
    11. **Slice and serve.**
