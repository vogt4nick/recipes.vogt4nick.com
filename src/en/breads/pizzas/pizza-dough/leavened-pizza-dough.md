=== "Ingredients"

    | Ingredient        | Leaven | Dough | Final | Final (%) |
    | :---------------- | -----: | ----: | ----: | --------: |
    | White Wheat Flour |  100 g | 200 g | 300 g |     100 % |
    | Wheat Starter     |   10 g |       |       |           |
    | Water             |   60 g | 150 g | 210 g |      70 % |
    | Salt              |        | 8.4 g | 8.4 g |     2.8 % |

    !!! info "Yields one Detroit-style pizza dough."

=== "Directions"

    1. **Mix starter.** Measure warm water into a sealable container, about 40ºC
        (104ºF). Add starter to water and stir. Mix in flour. Seal container and
        rest in a warm spot for at least 8 hours.
    2. **Autolyse.** Mix the flour and water until just incorporated. Cover and rest
        45 minutes.
    3. **Mix.** Measure warm water into a mixing bowl. Stir in salt until dissolved.
        Add leaven and mix by hand using the
        [pincer method](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoY7CPw0E1s). Add autolysed
        dough and continue using the pincer method to mix into a homogeneous mass.
        Verify dough temperature is about 27ºC (80ºF). Rest 20 minutes.
    4. **Knead.** Transfer to a lightly-floured work surface. Knead 60 seconds until
        the skin of the dough is very smooth. Return dough ball seam-side down to
        the original, lightly-oiled dough tub. Cover tightly with plastic wrap.
    5. **First rise.** Rest dough 3 hours between 70ºF-74ºF (21ºC-23ºC).
    6. **Shape dough.** Transfer dough to floured surface. Dust top of dough with
        flour. Gently shape dough into a medium-tight ball. Place dough ball on
        lightly floured plate with enough room to expand. Lightly flour top of
        dough balls. Cover with plastic wrap.
    7. **Second rise.** Rest 4 hours between 70ºF-74ºF (21ºC-23ºC).

    !!! info "Chill after second rise and bake later as desired."
