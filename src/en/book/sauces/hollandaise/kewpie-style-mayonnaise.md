---
title: Kewpie-style mayonnaise
---
=== "Ingredients"
    * 120 g Mayonnaise
    * 1/4 t Kosher Salt
    * 3/4 t White Sugar
    * 1 t Dijon Mustard
    * Hot Sauce, to taste
    * 1 t Lemon Juice
    * 2 t Rice Vinegar

    !!! info "Yields about 120 g mayonnaise."

=== "Directions"
    Combine ingredients and mix well. Refrigerate to store.
