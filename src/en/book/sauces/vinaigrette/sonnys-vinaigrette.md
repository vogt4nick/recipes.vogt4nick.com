---
title: Sonny's vinaigrette
---
=== "Ingredients"
    * 20 g White Wine Vinegar
    * 20 g Lemon Juice
    * 1/2 t Sea Salt
    * 1 T Mustard
    * 20 g Honey
    * 120 g Neutral Oil
    * 150 g Olive Oil

=== "Directions"
    Combine ingredients in a sealable jar. Shake vigorously to emulsify.
