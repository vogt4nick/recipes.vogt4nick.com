---
title: Vinaigrette
---
=== "Ingredients"
    * 30 g Dijon Mustard
    * 30 g White Wine Vinegar
    * 90 g Olive Oil
    * Kosher Salt
    * Black Peppercorn, ground

=== "Directions"
    Combine dressing ingredients in a sealable jar. Shake vigorously to combine.
