=== "Ingredients"
    60 g Balsamic Vinegar
    1 T Dijon Mustard
    1 T Honey
    1 Garlic Clove, minced
    1/2 t Sea Salt
    1/4 t Black Peppercorn, ground
    180 g Olive Oil

=== "Directions"
    Combine ingredients in a sealable jar. Shake vigorously to emulsify.
