=== "Ingredients"
    * 60 g Whiskey
    * 30 g Sweet Vermouth
    * 1 Dash Bitters (optional)
    * For Garnish
        * 1 Maraschino Cherry
        * 5 g Cherry Juice

??? tip "Pair with savory, meaty entrees."
    e.g. [B.O.C. Monsieur](../../handhelds/boc-monsieur.md), [Sauerbraten](../../meats/beef/sauerbraten.md).
