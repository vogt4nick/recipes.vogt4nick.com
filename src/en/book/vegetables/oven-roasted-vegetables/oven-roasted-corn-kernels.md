=== "Ingredients"
    * Corn Kernels
    * Neutral Oil
    * Kosher Salt
    * Black Peppercorn, ground

=== "Directions"
    1. **Preheat oven** to 350ºF (177ºC).
    2. **Prepare corn kernels** on a foiled baking tray.
    3. **Roast corn kernels** 15 minutes at 350ºF, turning halfway to roast them evenly.
