=== "Ingredients"
    * 120 g Plain Yogurt
    * 120 g Milk
    * 40 g Chia Seeds
    * 1 t White Granulated Sugar
    * 1/4 t Salt
    * Berries (optional)

    !!! info "Yields 1 serving."

=== "Directions"
    1. **Mix** to combine all ingredients in a sealable container.
    2. **Chill** 4-12 hours.
    3. **Serve** with berries mixed in.
