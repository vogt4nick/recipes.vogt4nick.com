=== "Ingredients"
    * 3 T Dried Sage Leaves, ground
    * 2 T Dried Thyme Leaves, ground
    * 2 T Dried Rosemary Leaves, ground
    * 1 T Dried Marjoram Leaves, ground
    * 1/2 t Nutmeg, ground
    * 1/2 t Black Peppercorn, ground

=== "Directions"
    Combine ingredients and blend thoroughly.
