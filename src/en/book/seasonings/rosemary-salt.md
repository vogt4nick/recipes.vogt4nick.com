=== "Ingredients"
    * 250 g Kosher Salt
    * 20 g Garlic
    * 16 g Fresh Rosemary Leaves
    * 8 g Fresh Sage Leaves
    * 3 g Lemon Zest

=== "Directions"
    Combine ingredients and blend thoroughly.
