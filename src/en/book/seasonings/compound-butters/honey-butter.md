=== "Ingredients"
    * 2 T Unsalted Butter, softened
    * 1 t Honey
    * 1 t Powdered Sugar
    * 1/8 t Kosher Salt

    !!! info "Ingredients yield about 3 T."

=== "Directions"
    Blend ingredients into a homogenous mixture.
