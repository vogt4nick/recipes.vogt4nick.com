---
title: Bulgur
---
=== "Ingredients"
    * 250 g Water
    * 100 g Bulgur
    * 1/2 T Olive Oil
    * 1/4 t Kosher Salt

=== "Directions"
    1. **Cook.** Add ingredients to saucepan. Bring to boil, cover, reduce to very low heat, and simmer 12 minutes.
    2. **Rest.** Remove from heat and rest 10 minutes, covered.
    3. **Season** with salt and olive oil.
