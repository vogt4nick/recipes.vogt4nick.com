---
title: Bulgur
---
=== "Ingredients"
    * 100 g Bulgur
    * 400 g Water, boiling
    * 1 t Butter
    * 1/4 t Salt

=== "Directions"
    Add bulgur, butter, and salt to saucepan. Add boiling water, and cook, uncovered.

    Fine bulgur will be tender in 10-15 minutes, medium in 15-20 minutes, and coarse bulgur in 20-25 minutes.
