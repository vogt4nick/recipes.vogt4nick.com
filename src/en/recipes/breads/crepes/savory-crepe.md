=== "Ingredients"
    * 4 [Crepes](index.md)
    * Mustard
    * Ham
    * Melty Cheese

=== "Directions"
    Spread mustard and layer ham and cheese. Cook in a preheated pan until cheese is melty. Fold crepe and serve.
