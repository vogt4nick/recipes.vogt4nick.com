=== "Ingredients"

    - 2 T Medium-Grain Salt
    - 1/2 t Baking Soda
    - 900 g Starchy Potatoes, peeled, quartered
    - 80 g Cooking Fat
    - 3 sprigs Rosemary Leaves
    - 1/2 T Black Peppercorn, ground
    - 1/2 T Medium-Grain Salt
    - For Serving
        - 90 g Sour Cream (optional)

=== "Directions"

    1. **Prepare materials.** Preheat oven to 450ºF (232ºC). Prepare a foiled
        sheetpan.
    1. **Boil potatoes.** Add potatoes, salt, baking soda, and water (enough to
        cover the mixture) to stockpan. Stir. Boil 6 minutes until nearly
        cooked-through. Strain\*\*.
    1. **Dry potatoes.** Rest potatoes 15 minutes until done steaming.
    1. **Season potatoes** with spices and oil.
    1. **Roast potatoes.** Move to prepared sheetpan. Spread evenly so no potatoes
        are touching. Roast 50 minutes at 450ºF, turning every 20 minutes.
    1. **Serve** with sour cream for dipping.

    ??? warning "Do not drain in a fine mesh colander."

        We want to keep the dry, starchy outsides of the potatoes. These will crisp and
        help the texture. A fine colander will grate these off.
