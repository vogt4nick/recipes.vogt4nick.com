=== "Ingredients"

    - Bell Peppers, whole

=== "Directions"

    1. **Preheat oven** to 350ºF (177ºC).
    1. **Prepare peppers** on a foiled baking tray.
    1. **Bake peppers** 60 minutes at 350ºF, turning every 15-20 minutes to roast
        them evenly.
    1. **Cool** to room-temperature.
    1. **Peel and seed** bell peppers.
