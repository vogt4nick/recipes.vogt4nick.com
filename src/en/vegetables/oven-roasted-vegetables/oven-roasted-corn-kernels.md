=== "Ingredients"

    - Corn Kernels
    - Neutral Oil
    - Medium-Grain Salt
    - Black Peppercorn, ground

=== "Directions"

    1. **Preheat oven** to 350ºF (177ºC).
    1. **Prepare corn kernels** on a foiled baking tray.
    1. **Roast corn kernels** 15 minutes at 350ºF, turning halfway to roast them
        evenly.
