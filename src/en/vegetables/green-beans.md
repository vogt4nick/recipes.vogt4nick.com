=== "Ingredients"
    * Kosher Salt
    * 200 g Green Beans, trimmed
    * 15 g Unsalted Butter
    * 1/4 t Garlic Powder
    * 1/4 t Black Peppercorn, ground

=== "Directions"
    1. **Parboil.** Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add green beans and cook 2 minutes. Drain.
    2. **Season.** Over medium-low heat, add butter, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Stir to coat and cook 1–2 minutes until butter is melted and beans are well seasoned.
    3. **Rest** until ready to eat.
