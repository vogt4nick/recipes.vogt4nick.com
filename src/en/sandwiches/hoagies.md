=== "Ingredients"

    - 1 Sausage
    - 1 Hoagie Bun
    - 50 g Mozzarella Cheese
    - [Dry Italian Seasoning](../seasonings/spice-blends/italian-herbs.md)

    !!! info "Yields 1 hoagie."

=== "Directions"

    1. **Preheat oven** to 200ºC (400ºF).
    2. **Cook sausage.**
    3. **Turn on broiler.**
    4. **Fill hoagies.** Cut the bun lengthwise but not all the way through. Fill
        bun with cooked sausage. Top sandwich with mozzarella and seasoning.
    5. **Broil hoagies** about 3 minutes until cheese is melted.
