---
title: Crème fraîche
---
=== "Ingredients"
    * 500 g Heavy Cream
    * 50 g Cultured Buttermilk

=== "Directions"
    1. **Mix** ingredients together.
    2. **Rest** 24 hours in a warm spot until thick (75 F or 22 C).
    3. **Chill** 24 hours before using.

!!! info "Store up to two weeks chilled."
