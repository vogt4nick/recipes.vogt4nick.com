## Cooking Fats

| Cooking Fat            | Smoke Point |
|:-----------------------|:------------|
| Extra Virgin Olive Oil | 325F (160C)
| Butter                 | 350F (175C)
| Coconut Oil            | 350F (175C)
| Pork Fat               | 375F (190C)
| Canola Oil             | 400F (205C)

See [*Cooking Fats and Oils*](https://www.skillsyouneed.com/ps/fats-oils.html) for more information.
